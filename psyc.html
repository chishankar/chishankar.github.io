Intro to Learning:

  • Learning: is the process of accumulating knowledge via experience & practice, an enduring change in behavior as a result of practice & experience
    Two types : Declaritive and Prodecural
    Declaritive refers to past events, meanings of concepts, and significance of things
    Proceadural refers to the skills that learned, such as playing an instrument, stimulus -> response
    Behaviors are based on antecedent stimulus, without conscious reflection. Does not require conscious processing like Declaritive memory. This makes this type of learning more efficient.
  • To study learning, we look for changes in an organisms behavior (Any activity of an organism that can be measured)
    However, change in behavior may not reflect learning in part due to
    • Fatigue If the animal is too tired to continue, muscle fatigue
    • Maturation Once the animal begins to mature, (Giraffe grass/tree example)
    • Movtivation Once the animals thirst/hunger is satisfied
    Latent Learning: is a type of learning that is dormant until such time as it is needed. (Bus/school example)
    Devaluation: when a person attributes themselves, an object, or another personal as completley flawed and negative
